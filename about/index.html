About Quantora Analytics

We architect production-ready AI systems designed for your team's long-term independence.

Quantora Analytics is an enterprise AI consulting company that bridges the gap between AI experimentation and production. Founded by Shashank Pandey, we help teams ship reliable AI systems, resilient pipelines, and intelligent applications that drive measurable business outcomes.

Our story

Quantora Analytics was founded by practitioners who spent years inside product, data, and engineering teams at high‑growth companies. We saw the same pattern repeat: promising prototypes that never made it to production, and dashboards that drifted away from the questions leaders actually cared about.

We created Quantora to do things differently—combining deep technical expertise with product thinking, change management, and a relentless focus on business outcomes.

How we think

  • Production first: We design solutions that can be monitored, owned, and evolved.
  • Human‑centered: Analysts, engineers, and stakeholders are part of the design process.
  • Vendor‑neutral: We recommend tools because they fit, not because they’re trendy.
  • Transparent: Clear documentation, explainable models, and shared metrics.
