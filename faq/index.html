Process & Timeline

Our 5-phase process: (1) Discovery & Data Audit — align on KPIs, assess data, map architecture. (2) Architecture Design — system design, model selection, API contracts. (3) Prototype & Validate — working POC with real data. (4) Build & Deploy — production engineering with CI/CD and monitoring. (5) Knowledge Transfer — documentation, training, handover.

POC/proof-of-concept: 2–3 weeks. Production MVP: 6–10 weeks. Full enterprise deployment: 3–6 months. We use 2-week sprints with demo checkpoints so you see working software throughout the engagement, not just at the end.

Yes. All projects include 30 days of post-deployment support. Extended support retainers (3, 6, 12 months) are available and include model monitoring, drift alerts, updates, and a dedicated Slack channel with <24h response SLA.
