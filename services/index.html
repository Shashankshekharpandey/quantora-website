Generative AI Programs

LLM strategy, LLMOps engineering, and Hugging Face acceleration.

We help teams launch GenAI initiatives that are production-grade from day one—combining pragmatic use-case design, safety reviews, and the infrastructure required to operate large language models at scale.

LLM Discovery & Roadmaps

Identify high-leverage copilot, agent, or automation opportunities, evaluate GPT-4/4o, Claude, Llama 3, Mistral, and Hugging Face models, then define data, security, and governance requirements.

  • Prompt libraries & conversation blueprints
  • Safety, privacy, and red-teaming plans
  • Evaluation harnesses for hallucination & bias

LLMOps Foundations

Stand up tooling that treats LLM workflows like software: versioned prompts, automated testing, observability for latency/cost/quality, and continuous delivery across multiple model providers.

  • Prompt/version registries & GitOps
  • CI/CD pipelines, canary deploys, guardrails
  • Telemetry for drift, toxicity, and usage

Hugging Face Engineering

Fine-tune, distill, and deploy open-weight models using Transformers, PEFT, and LoRA with secure hosting on SageMaker, Vertex AI, Azure, or self-managed GPU clusters.

  • Dataset curation & synthetic data generation
  • Quantization (GGUF, ONNX, TensorRT) & caching
  • Responsible AI & compliance documentation
