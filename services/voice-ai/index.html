The Problem

Manual calling operations don't scale — and they're expensive.

  • Human agents cost $15–30/hour with limited coverage hours
  • Inconsistent quality across reps — no standardization
  • No real-time quality auditing at scale
  • Peak traffic causes long wait times and dropped calls
  • CRM updates are manual and error-prone

Our Solution

Production Voice AI with sub-300ms latency and human-like quality.

  • Real-time STT with Deepgram (97%+ accuracy)
  • LLM-powered conversation logic with context memory
  • Neural TTS with ElevenLabs for natural voice output
  • Automated CRM updates and call summaries
  • Sentiment analysis and escalation routing

What We Build

Voice AI solutions for every enterprise use case.

Inbound Customer Service

24/7 AI agents handling customer queries, account management, troubleshooting, and complaint resolution — with seamless human handoff.

  • Intent classification and routing
  • Knowledge base integration
  • Escalation to human agents
  • Post-call summary and CRM update

Outbound Sales & Qualification

Automated outbound calling for lead qualification, appointment scheduling, and follow-up campaigns with personalized conversations at scale.

  • Lead scoring and prioritization

  • Disposition tagging and reporting

AI Call Quality Auditing

Automated analysis of 100% of calls for compliance, script adherence, sentiment, and outcome prediction — replacing manual spot-checking.

  • Sentiment and emotion analysis
